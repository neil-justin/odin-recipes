Katsudon

Katsudon from Boku no Hero Academia

Do you know that...

  • The pork is breaded and fried before being layered on top
  • The dish is best known from the anime Yuri!! On Ice, where Yuri Katsuki eats a bowl every time he wins a figure skater competition.
  • Katsudon is Deku's (from My Hero Academia) favorite food

Ingredients

  • 2 center-cut (boneless pork chops, pounded down to a centimeter thick)
  • salt and pepper
  • flour (for dusting)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup panko
  • oil (for frying)
  • 1/2 cup dashi stock or chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Mirin
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 medium onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 servings steamed white rice
  • 1 scallion (chopped)
